Sandwiched Sandwich

Description

It's, like, really, really good.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bread
  • 2 Baby Carrots (must be siblings)

Steps

  1. Brush your teeth. Dental hygiene is important.
  2. Persuade Bread to let you slice it into halves. (You might need to pay it a pretty penny.)
  3. Snack on your baby carrots, one at a time.
  4. Call a loved one and share a message of affirmation.
  5. Enjoy!
